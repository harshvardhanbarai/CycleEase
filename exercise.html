Diet Recommendations

A balanced diet is crucial for women during their menstrual cycle. It helps in managing symptoms like bloating, fatigue, and cramps.

Iron-rich foods: Incorporate foods like spinach, lentils, and red meat to replenish the iron lost during menstruation.

Calcium and Vitamin D: Include dairy products, fortified cereals, and fatty fish to strengthen bones and alleviate cramps.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Foods like flaxseeds, chia seeds, and walnuts help reduce inflammation and menstrual pain.

Hydration: Drink plenty of water to prevent dehydration and bloating during your cycle.
