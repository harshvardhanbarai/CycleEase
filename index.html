Government Schemes for Women

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP): This scheme aims to address the declining child sex ratio and promote the survival, protection, and education of the girl child. It targets raising awareness and improving the efficiency of welfare services intended for girls. More details here.
https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/bbbp

One Stop Centre (Sakhi): These centers provide support to women affected by violence. They offer services such as medical aid, police assistance, legal aid, and psychological counseling under one roof.

wadhar Greh: This scheme provides shelter, food, and rehabilitation services to women in distress, including survivors of violence, widows, and those deserted by their families. It aims to help women regain their dignity.
https://www.india.gov.in/schemes-women

Ujjawala Scheme: Focuses on the prevention of trafficking and the rehabilitation of victims through rescue, reintegration, and repatriation.
https://vikaspedia.in/energy/policy-support/pradhan-mantri-ujjwala-yojana#:~:text=Pradhan%20Mantri%20Ujjwala%20Yojana%20is,2016%20in%20Ballia%2C%20Uttar%20Pradesh

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY): A maternity benefit program offering financial assistance of ₹6,000 to pregnant and lactating mothers in three installments. The scheme is designed to encourage safe motherhood and healthcare.
https://pmmvy.wcd.gov.in/
