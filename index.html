Government Schemes for Women

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP): This scheme aims to address the declining child sex ratio and promote the survival, protection, and education of the girl child.
Read more

One Stop Centre (Sakhi): These centers provide support to women affected by violence, offering services such as medical aid, legal aid, and psychological counseling.
Read more

Swadhar Greh: Provides shelter, food, and rehabilitation services to women in distress, including survivors of violence.
Read more

Ujjawala Scheme: Focuses on the prevention of trafficking and the rehabilitation of victims through rescue and reintegration.
Read more

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY): A maternity benefit program offering financial assistance to pregnant and lactating mothers.
